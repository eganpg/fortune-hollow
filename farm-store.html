What's available

Straight from the farm.

Everything here comes from animals on this property. The milk is from Nan. The eggs are from hens who live outside on pasture. Nothing is factory-farmed or store-bought.

Jersey milk is richer than anything you'll find at a grocery store — higher butterfat, creamier, and you can taste the difference. Nan is milked every morning at 6 am and every evening at 6 pm. What's in the cooler was pulled this morning.
